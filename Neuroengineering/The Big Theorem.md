# Geometric problem
- Suppose we have a list of vectors $\vec{a_{1}},...,\vec{a_n}$
- Is the span, or linear combinations, of these vectors the entire space $\mathbb{R}^n$?
- In other words, is $\span\{\vec{a_{1}},...,\vec{a_n}\} = \mathbb{R}^n$?
